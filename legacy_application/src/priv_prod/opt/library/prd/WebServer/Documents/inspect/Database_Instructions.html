Safety Inspections Database

 

The Superintendent of each park will need to choose who will be in charge of entering safety information for their park.  Any safety related report generated by the park needs to be recorded through the database.  The park still needs to send copies of all PR-63s, audits, and other safety paperwork to their District Superintendent.  Original documents need to be retained at the park level.

 

Instructions For Entering Data:

  • • Once in the Safety Inspections Database, click on the “Select Action” dropdown menu at the top left corner of the screen and choose “Enter Safety Activities”. 

 

  • • If this is your first time entering anything into the database, you will need to click the “Add” button.  If your park already has information in the database, you will need to go down to the table and click on your park’s acronym in the “Parkcode” column. 

 

  • • You will see a list of all of the routine, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and yearly activities listed at the top of the screen.  Click on the proper activity for the information you are entering. 

 

  • • Choose the date of the activity from the dropdown menus and hit “Add”. 

 

  • • A row will populate with all of the information you just entered.  Go to the “Comments” column and click on “Edit”. 

 

  • • If this was entered into the PR-63 database, type the PR-63 number into the first box. Type any comments or relevant information into the large box.  Hit the “Add” button to the right. 

 

  • • Go to the “File Upload” section at the bottom of the page and upload a scanned PDF (do not upload Word Documents) of any paperwork and then click “Add File”.  More than one document can be uploaded if necessary.
